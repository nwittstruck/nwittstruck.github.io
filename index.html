About adBuster

adBuster is a simple, yet powerful free Ad-Blocker for iOS. Load websites in Safari faster, safe mobile data and increase your battery life!

Features

  • Default blacklist with over 2500 blocked ad companies
  • Optimised for performance and low battery usage
  • Block ads in 3rd Party Apps using Safari as well
  • Extend your blacklist by adding your own URL filters
  • Easy to use whitelist to disable the adblocker on your favourite websites
  • Automatic update of rules - no need to reload the content blocker in the settings
  • Add new entries to the blacklist using the convenient Safari Extension (coming soon)
  • iCloud support to synchronise black and whitelist across all your devices (coming soon)
